Napa Eagle Wineries

Today, Napa Valley is known as one of the wine capitals of the world - arguably more acclaimed than even the Bordeaux region in France. However, it wasn't always this way. In a 1976 blind tasting competition later to be known as the famed Judgment of Paris, American Cabs and Chardonnays won, topping those of the French variety. This outraged the French wine industry, but from that day thereafter, America's Napa Valley was established unquestionably as a formidable producer of world class wines.

It was to this history and spirit of American pride that we, Napa Eagle Winery, entered the Cabernet Sauvignon scene. Sourcing our grapes from the heart of Napa Valley from vineyards such as George III, Stagecoach, and Broken Rock, we accept only the best of the best.

Our story, our legacy

Winemaking has been a industry traditionally dominated by Europeans and people originally of European descent. As ethnic Chinese, we're here to challenge that. Our ancestors came here and built the railroads, providing the fundamental infrastructure that California needed to thrive. And through the fruits of our labor, entire communities, cities, and industries rose along the beautiful bay, across the mountainous horizon, and of course - cradled within the luscious, prime vineyards of Napa Valley, California.

We're here to show the world that it's not just the Europeans and Americans who can produce world class wine. We're here to demonstrate that we can do it just as well or better. Taking our heritage to heart, we produce our wine with the ancient touch of the Chinese - and you'll taste it in every single drop.
